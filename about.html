About ME!

Hi, My name is Woody.

Welcome to my website! My name is Woody Tai, and I am a current High School student with a diverse range of interests. My strong curiosity for understanding how things work, especially in the realm of technology or science. My albeit short journey has taken me on exciting paths, from web development to chemistry, I hope we can work together to forge our future.

My Schooling has played a vital role in shaping my knowledge base. I am studying at the Chinese Foundation Secondary School, where I excel in subjects like Chemistry, Biology and ICT(Information & Communication Technology). Tech as a whole has always fascinated me, teaching me the power of critical thinking, grasping principles and thinking out of the box.

Beyond just academics, I am in a variety of extracurricular activities(CCAs) to broaden my horizons and gain valuable experiences. As a General Officer in the Nominated Student Council, I successfully managed and grew our social media presence, doubling the follower count. In the Computer Club, I served as the Chairperson, organizing engaging events and conducting media-based workshops, and acting as primary liaison with the Robotics Club to conduct cross curriculum education. Additionally, my role as a Vice-President of the Career and Life Planning Team allowed me to collaborate and co-organising with external organizations such as a talk co-hosted Dr Patrick YUE an Associate Programme Director for BSc (Hons) in Applied Biology at HKBU, and various other activities tailored to suit the targeted students. I strive not only to serve in my own local school and bridging the gaps in Senior Secondary to University, I also work with other Schools and fellow students who share the same vision as mine, I am dedicated to serving my own community and a strong believer in youth empowerment, my experience and innovative thoughts have given me the pleasure of being elected the President of the Hong Kong Assocoiation of Youth Leaders (formally Joint School Leadership Society) of which I organise talks, seminars and additional activities to further promote youth leadership and facilitate talks between the aspiring youth of our generation. As the General Secretary of the Hong Kong Joint School Association of Secondary Students I have had the pleasure of putting a principle I firmly believe in "Work Hard, Play Hard". Beyond leadership and fun, I occasionally dabble in the arts. By serving as the Art Director of the Hong Kong Island Senior Students Association I believe I can elevate not just degree of art, but also marketability of such works, Serving as the art director allowed me to perfect my Photoshop and Lightroom skills, which are essential in our modern world filled and entrenched in social media.

One of my achievements that paved my experience with Diplomacy and Effective Communication is being the Debate Captain of the English Debate Team. By leading my team to competitions with different schools, I have truly learnt a lot of techniques and tips for demonstrating and speaking with confidence.
Additionally, as the Head Delegate to the Mission to MUN, has taught me invaluable lessons relating to international communications and most importantly negating to find a middle ground both sides can agree to — An invaluable asset in terms of leading those around me.

Furthermore, I spent my F3 (Year 9)'s summer working as a Research Intern at The Chinese University of Hong Kong(CUHK)— Faculty of Science, recruited by Dr. Lam Hon-Ming and under the supervision of his PostDoctoral Fellow Dr. Yung Wai-Shing Vincent. Additionally I am dedicated to learning outside the classroom. Honing my skills in Full-Stack web Dev through courses at Preface and practical projects.
