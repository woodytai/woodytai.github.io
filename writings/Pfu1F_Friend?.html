Friends (I hope)

Last semester we were in the same form group, we both hung out but we were overworked and had some unresolved conflicts, so we took a small break, what we both didn't realise was that the break was a year too long.

Moreover, as the new semester commenced, our form groups were once more rearranged. Just when I thought our relationship began to warm, This girl that neither of us was close with, wanted to play matchmaker and decided to ship us together, which made our relationship sour once again, I tried to find you only to be met with hostility, I tried to move on but everything hurts, our lives were way too over twined at that point, I couldn't bear to lose you, I messaged you over the month, only to have an annoyed response flash across your face.

Last Friday, we talked for the first time in over a month. Your eyes were tired from working alone, your soul agape. I could see your sorrow hidden behind that Boy Scout face. Hell, that could just be my delusions.

So, here I am, and there you are, finding me today. We chatted, not about the mess of insecurities brewing inside, but about work. It bugs me, you know? I've got a million things I want to say, but they all stay stuck in my head. All I could do was look into your eyes, feeling more hurt than ever, yet you seemed to miss it all.

Cursed are those who feel oceans but can only express a drop.

Then, in the rush between classes, you threw out the idea of us running for head boy together. My eyes lit up with hope, thinking, "Is this real?" You caught that glimmer, but I wonder if you sensed the mix of embarrassment and sadness I am so used to hiding behind my smile. Now, I'm torn. Should I be mad or glad that you're reaching out to me like this?
