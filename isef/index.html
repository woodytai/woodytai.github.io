  • What is an electrocatalyst
    Electrocatalyst (n.) is a substance that speeds up the rate of electrochemical reactions, reactions that involve the transfer of electrons.
    The activity of electrocatalysts can be evaluated from how much current density is generated, and therefore how fast a reaction is taking place at a give applied potential.
